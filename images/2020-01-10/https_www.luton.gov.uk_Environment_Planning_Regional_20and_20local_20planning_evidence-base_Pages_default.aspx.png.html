Luton Local Plan – Evidence base

Sub heading

This page lists and provides links to the evidence base behind the Luton Local Plan 2011-2031 and its subsequent review.
Page Content Extra 1
The documents which form the evidence for the Luton Local Plan are grouped by topic following the structure of the plan. If you click on the appropriate reference the document or relevant website will open up.

Further links may be added as appropriate as plan preparation progresses.
