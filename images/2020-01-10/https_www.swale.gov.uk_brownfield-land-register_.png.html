Brownfield Land Register

Following legislation which came into force in April 2017, the Council is required to prepare, maintain and publish a Brownfield Land Register which contains information on previously developed land that may be considered appropriate for residential development.

The register is to be split into 2 parts:

  • Part 1 is to include sites of 0.25 hectares and above which are considered available, suitable and achievable of residential development, in line with the criteria set out within the legislation
  • Part 2 is to include sites from part 1 which the Council has decided to take forwards and grant permission in principle for, having been through a publicity and consultation process

At the present time, the Council will not be taking any sites forwards for inclusion in Part 2 of the register, but this position will be reviewed annually.
