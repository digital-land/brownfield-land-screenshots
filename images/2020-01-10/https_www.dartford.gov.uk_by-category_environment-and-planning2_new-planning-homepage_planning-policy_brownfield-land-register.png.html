Brownfield Land Register

Dartford Borough, including Ebbsfleet, hosts a large amount of land that was disused or derelict, having previously been developed.

All Local Planning Authorities are tasked by the government with producing a 'Brownfield Land Register' of previously developed sites for potential housing use each year. The Register is not a Local Plan and does not contain planning policies.

The Brownfield Land Register for December 2019 is available in PDF form below:

  • Dartford Brownfield Land Register (December 2019)   PDF, 321.15 KB

A basic dataset for each site has been submitted to government, to make the information publicly available. The Register will be updated annually, and revised based on information from any new Strategic Housing Land Availability Assessment (SHLAA) of the Borough in 2019.

The Brownfield Land Register data is available below in CSV and shape file format:

  • Brownfield Land Register data in CSV Format CSV, 12.43 KB
  • Download the Shapefiles ZIP, 28.81 KB

Access the Brownfield Land Register site plans.
