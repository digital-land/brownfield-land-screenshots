Brownfield Land Register

The Brownfield Land Register Review for Wirral was re-published in December 2018.

Part 1 Sites (csv file)
Part 1 Sites (maps)

The register will be monitored and reviewed during 2019.

Part 1 of the Brownfield Land Register for Wirral was first published, following public consultation, in December 2017.

The deadline for comments on the sites proposed to be included expired on 6 December 2017.

Further information can be obtained from forwardplanning@wirral.gov.uk
