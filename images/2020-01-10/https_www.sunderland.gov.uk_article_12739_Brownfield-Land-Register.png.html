Brownfield Land Register

  1. Home
  2. Residents
  3. Planning and building control
  4. Planning Policy

The Brownfield Land Register is a schedule of those previously developed 'brownfield' sites that have been assessed to be suitable, available and potentially achievable for housing development within the next 15 years.

Sunderland City Council has not at the present time proposed granting planning 'Permission in Principle' (Part 2) for any of the sites included on the Brownfield Land Register.
