Brownfield Register

The 2017 Regulations introduced the requirement for all local planning authorities in England to prepare, maintain and publish registers of previously developed land within their area that they consider appropriate for residential development that

  • has an area of at least 0.25 hectares or is capable of supporting at least 5 dwellings;
  • is suitable for residential development;
  • is available for residential development; and
  • is achievable for residential development
