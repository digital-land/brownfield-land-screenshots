Brownfield land register

We maintain a register of brownfield sites in the Harlow area that may be suitable for residential development.

The brownfield land register is made up of 2 parts:

  • part 1 is a list of brownfield sites in the Harlow district that may be suitable for housing
  • part 2 is a subset of part 1 and is a list of sites that have been granted planning permission in principle

Sites that are included in part 1 of the register will not necessarily be granted permission in principle.

We do not have any sites on the part 2 register.

The draft part 1 of the register is available for comment.

  • Brownfield land register - part 1 (csv)
  • Brownfield land register - part 1 (xlsx)

If you want to comment on any of the sites included in the list or if you think other sites should be included then you can contact us.
