Brownfield Land Register

We have prepared a Brownfield Land Register according to the Town and Country Planning (Brownfield Land Register) Regulations 2017. The register gives details of previously developed land in the borough that is appropriate for residential development:

  • Southwark Brownfield register 2017 (xlsx, 94kb)

Site boundaries for land on the register can be viewed on our online Brownfield Register map.
