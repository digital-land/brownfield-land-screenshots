Sandwell Brownfield Register

The Town and country Planning (Brownfield Land Register) Regulations 2017 and the Town and Country Planning (Permission in Principle) Order 2017 require local authorities to prepare and maintain registers of brownfield land that is suitable for residential development.  

The current Sandwell Brownfield Register consists of sites where the former use was brownfield i.e. excluding open space and garden land sites.  The Council doesn't intend to grant 'Permission in Principle' (Part 2) for any of the sites at this stage.  Site information and site boundaries can be viewed on the Council's Brownfield Land Register.
