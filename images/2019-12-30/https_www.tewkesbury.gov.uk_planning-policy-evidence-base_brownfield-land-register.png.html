About the planning policy evidence base

The planning policy evidence base supports the preparation of the local plan and other policy documents. A number of studies have been produced, in some cases undertaken with or by other authorities and consultants.

A shared policy evidence base can be found on the Joint Core Strategy website. Below are reports, plans and studies unique to Tewkesbury Borough Council.
